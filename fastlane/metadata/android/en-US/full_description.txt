Schautrack is the native Android app for Schautrack, a self-hostable, open-source, AI-powered calorie tracker for you and your friends.

Track what you eat, set daily goals, and keep an eye on your weight, with optional AI nutrition estimation from a single food photo. The app wraps the Schautrack web app in a native shell and adds camera access for food scanning and barcode lookups.

By default the app connects to schautrack.com, but you can point it at your own server right from the login screen, so your data stays wherever you choose to host it.

Features:

* Calorie entry tracking with daily goals
* AI-powered nutrition estimation from food photos (OpenAI, Claude, or Ollama)
* Barcode scanning via OpenFoodFacts
* Weight tracking in kg or lbs
* Daily notes and recurring to-dos with streak tracking
* Account linking to share data with friends
* Two-factor authentication (TOTP) with backup codes
* Real-time updates across devices
* Use the public instance, or self-host your own

Schautrack is free and open source, licensed under the AGPL-3.0. The server is a small Go application backed by PostgreSQL. Source code for both the app and the server is linked below.

Note: by default the app talks to the hosted instance at schautrack.com, and the AI food estimation feature relies on external providers (OpenAI or Claude) unless you configure a local model. You can run the entire stack yourself if you prefer to keep everything in-house.
